Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the account settings page and click on "Forgot Password". Follow the instructions to reset your password.

How can I refund a game?

You can request a refund through the purchase history page. Click on the game you want to refund and select "Request a Refund". Follow the instructions to complete your request.

Contact Us
